Who is Sotschi?

If you can dream it, you can do it.

Biography




Sotschi is a Northern Italian newcomer bass music producer and artist, featured on Trap Cosmos, Artist Maniacs and many other music channels. His tracks are very much inspired by the artist ILLENIUM while his live sets are a mixture beetween Virtual Riot, Skrillex, Dj Snake, Eptic and Subsurface.

In early 2018 the first tracks by Sotschi appeared on Soundcloud, Spotify, Deezer and many more. While Sotschi was hospitalized at the end of 2018 due to a brain disease, in 2019 he played all along his home region of South Tyrol.

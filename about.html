Biography




Starting in early 2018, Sotschi is a young Italian music producer featured on AirwavesmusicTV, Trap Cosmos, 1dobrazil and other big blogs and music channels. With numerous releases on labels like LoudKult, TrapTownRecords and Kardo Records, Sotschi is counting more than 500.000 streams across all streaming plattforms.

With plenty of live performances in diverse clubs and festivals all across his hometown, Sotschi is acting as a bass music artist. His music can be described as a mixture of Virtual Riot, Skrillex, DJ Snake, Eptic, Zomboy, Wooli and other big names in the music industry, which means he plays across the genres Dubstep, Harddance, Future Bass, Drum n Bass and Bass House.

Story

Up&Down

In the dynamic world of electronic dance music, Sotschi and JeLa, formed a powerful collaboration that would leave a lasting impact. Sotschi, a renowned music producer and DJ, had garnered international recognition with his energetic releases and support from top EDM artists. JeLa, a gifted singer-songwriter from NYC, had already made her mark as a prolific songwriter and had recently stepped into the spotlight as a performer. When Sotschi discovered JeLa's captivating vocals and emotive melodies, he couldn't resist reaching out to her for a collaboration. Excited by the opportunity to work with him, JeLa eagerly accepted. In Sotschi's state-of-the-art studio, their creative synergy ignited, and they embarked on a journey to craft something extraordinary. Their hard work and passion culminated in their track "Up & Down", a fusion of Sotschi's vibrant beats and JeLa's heartfelt lyrics.

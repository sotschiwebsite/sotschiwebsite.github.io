Everything you need to know about Sotschi and his personality

Sotschi is a music producer and DJ.

Sotschi was born in Bolzano, a city located in Northern Italy with a population of around 100,000 residents.

Sotschi, born on August 15th, 2001, is currently 21 years old.

Sotschi's Remix for Armen Paul's and Oiliva Reid's track In Control stands as his most popular song, capturing the hearts of listeners across various music platforms. Following closely behind in second place is his collaboration with Tarik Asadi titled Coming Up. Together, these two tracks have amassed an impressive total of over 4 Million Streams.

Yes, Sotschi has indeed performed at notable venues, both in Northern Italy and internationally. A key highlight of his career was his performance at the esteemed Noa Beach Club at Zrce Beach in Croatia, which is recognized as one of the top 28 clubs globally.

Throughout his career, Sotschi has engaged in several collaborations with various talented artists. Some of the artists he has collaborated with include Armen Paul, Olivia Reid, Robbie Rosen, Jessica Lattman, Rily Shay, Sofia Derobertis, Tarik Asadi, Lena Luisa, Jonas Oberstaller, Cody Dunstall, and Atkin$.

In terms of his calm and catchy releases, however, his “bassy futurehouse” live sets are very energetic and euphoric.

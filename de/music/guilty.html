Guilty

Sotschi X Sofia Derobertis

For his new debut single “Guilty”, the musician from Sotschi got the 16-year-old New Yorker, Sofia Derobertis, to the microphone. Together with her and the American Idol finalist Robbie Rosen, the South Tyrolean wrote a very contemporary song text that appeals to all those for whom cheating plays a certain role in life.
